
# 🌐 Digital Literacy Project — Ansh Yadav

This project was completed as part of the **CSE0001 - Digital Literacy** course. As a **Student Digital Ambassador**, I explored various digital tools, platforms, and practices essential for effective and responsible use of technology. The project focuses on building digital awareness, communication skills, and understanding cyber safety.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Understand the concept of digital literacy  
- Build a professional online presence  
- Explore coding and collaboration platforms  
- Learn email etiquette and communication  
- Gain awareness about cybercrime and safety

## 📝 Tasks Overview

Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic  
Created an infographic using Canva explaining digital literacy and its importance in a simple and visually appealing way.

Task 2: Student Digital Portfolio  
Developed and improved professional profiles on LinkedIn and GitHub to build a strong online presence.

Task 3: Coding & Collaboration  
Solved a beginner-level coding problem on HackerRank and created a Google Form to collect and analyze responses.

Task 4: Email Etiquette  
Drafted professional emails and created a checklist of social media do’s and don’ts to understand proper communication.

Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness  
Studied phishing as a cybercrime and created a case study along with a prevention checklist to understand online safety.
