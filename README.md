
# 🌐 Digital Literacy Project — Ansh Yadav

This project was completed as part of the **CSE0001 - Digital Literacy** course. As a **Student Digital Ambassador**, I explored various digital tools, platforms, and practices essential for effective and responsible use of technology. The project focuses on building digital awareness, communication skills, and understanding cyber safety.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Understand the concept of digital literacy  
- Build a professional online presence  
- Explore coding and collaboration platforms  
- Learn email etiquette and communication  
- Gain awareness about cybercrime and safety  

---


---

## 🚀 Tasks Overview

### 🟢 Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic
- Created using **Canva**
- Explained key concepts of digital literacy
- Focused on visual clarity and simplicity
- Included safe internet practices and awareness

---

### 🔵 Task 2: Student Digital Portfolio
- Built professional profiles on **LinkedIn & GitHub**
- Added skills, education, and headline
- Improved online presence and visibility
- Planned long-term profile growth strategy

---

### 🟣 Task 3: Coding & Collaboration
- Solved a coding problem on **HackerRank**
- Practiced logical thinking and problem-solving
- Created a **Google Form**
- Collected and analyzed responses

### 🟡 Task 4: Email Etiquette
- Wrote professional emails (formal communication)
- Learned structure of formal emails
- Created **Social Media Do’s & Don’ts checklist**
- Improved digital communication skills

---

### 🔴 Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness
- Studied **Phishing attacks**
- Understood real-world cyber threats
- Created a detailed case study
- Designed a prevention checklist for safety
