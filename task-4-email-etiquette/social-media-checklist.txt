DO’s:
- Use professional and respectful language online
- Think before posting or commenting
- Keep personal information private
- Verify information before sharing
- Maintain a positive and responsible digital presence

DON’Ts:
- Do not share fake or misleading information
- Avoid offensive or abusive language
- Do not post sensitive personal details
- Avoid cyberbullying or trolling
- Do not misuse or copy others’ content without permission
