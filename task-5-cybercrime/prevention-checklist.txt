Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist:

- Always check the sender’s email address before opening any message
- Do not click on suspicious or unknown links
- Avoid sharing personal or financial information through email or messages
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Carefully check website URLs before entering login details
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive transactions
- Keep your devices and applications updated regularly
- Be cautious of urgent or threatening messages asking for immediate action
- Report cybercrime incidents at https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Contact helpline number 1930 in case of financial fraud
